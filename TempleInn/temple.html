//* Rome

Rome, Italy

The Rome Italy Temple lies in northeast Rome near the Grande Raccordo Anulare. Dedicated in seven sessions held 10 March - 12 March, 2019 by Russell M. Nelson. It was the first temple built in Italy. The dedication of the Rome Italy Temple marked the first time in history the entire First Presidency and Quorum of the Twelve Apostles gathered in one location outside the United States.

//* Toronto

Toronto, Canada

The Toronto Ontario Temple was dedicated the 25 - 27 August, 1990 by Gordon B. Hinckley. It was the second temple built in Canada. A 105-foot spire emerges from the rooftop of the temple and it is surrounded by exquisitely landscaped grounds. Inscriptions on the exterior are in both French and English.

//* Kyiv

Kyiv, Ukraine

Dedicated 29 August 2010 by Thomas S Monson, the Kyiv Ukraine Temple was the first temple built in Ukraine and the former Soviet Union. The gleaming exterior is finished with Amarelo Macieira granite with quartzite crystals that reflect sunlight. In 2011, the Kyiv Temple was awarded first place for the best religious building constructed in Ukraine in 2010.
