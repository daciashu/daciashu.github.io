Discovering Soda Springs

In the summer of 1937 Soda Springs was drilling a well seeking a hot water source for a swimming pool but instead found a carbonated water source that sent a geyser spraying 100 feet into the air. The citizens capped the geyser and release the flow once an hour. You can’t fool mother nature, but in Soda Springs they have her working on a time clock.
